Vegan Walnut Dates Cake

Description

This is a rich and sugar-free vegan Cake. The dairy substitute here can be any plant-based milk of your choice. Here we will be using Almond Milk. This recipe will be using Medjool Dates as the sugar substitute. Finally, for a healthier option, we will be using Whole Wheat flour.

Ingredients

  • 20-25 Pitted Dates
  • 1 1/4 cup Hot Almond Milk
  • 1/2 cup Oil
  • 1 cup Whole Wheat Flour
  • 1 teaspoon Baking Soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon Baking Powder
  • 1/3 cup Walnuts

Steps

  1. Soak pitted dates in hot almond milk for about 2-3 hours until soften.
  2. Lightly dry roast the chopped Walnuts using a stove top and set aside.
  3. Preheat oven to 360 Degree F.
  4. Blend soaked dates until smooth. Add oil to the mixture and blend.
  5. Sift dry ingredients and add in the wet mixture. Mix until a smooth batter is achieved.
  6. Add 2/3 of the roasted walnuts in the mixture and mix using a spatula.
  7. Pour mixture to a greesed baking pan. Finally remaining roasted walnuts on top.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes or until skewer comes out clean.
  9. Let Cake cool before serving.
