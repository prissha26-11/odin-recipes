Vegan Tofu Chocolate Pudding

Description

This is a simple and delicious vegan friendly creamy chocolate pudding recipe. You will only need 5 ingredient for this recipe. Silken Tofu will be used as a dairy substitute. To ensure the chocolate used is Vegan we will opt for Dark Chocolate that has at least 60% cocoa content. Finally to sweeten this dessert we will be adding Maple Syrup.

This dessert is Dairy-free and Vegan. Additional toppings can be added to this pudding such as Raspberry, chocolate shavings etc.

Ingrediants

  • 500g Silken Tofu
  • 80g Dark Chocolate
  • 1 tablespoon Cocoa Powder
  • 2 tablespoon Maple Syrup
  • 1 pinch of Salt

Steps

  1. Melt dark chocolate using a heated water bath or microwave.
  2. Add silken tofu, cocoa powder, melted chocolate, maple syrup, salt in a food processor and blend until creamy.
  3. Divide pudidng mixture into 4 ramekins.
  4. Let them rest in the refrigerator for 2-3 hours.
  5. Add topping if desired before serving.
